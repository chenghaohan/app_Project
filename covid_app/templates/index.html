Introduction

COVID-19 is a contagious disease caused by severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2 (SARS-CoV-2). The first case was identified in Wuhan, China, in December 2019. The disease has since spread worldwide, leading to an ongoing pandemic. This dashboard visualizes the impact of COVID-19 on the world with emphasis on North America during the period of January 1, 2020 to January 31, 2021.

An interactive map of Canada has been plotted, which shows the number of cases, deaths and vaccines by province. An interactive world map has also been plotted, which shows the number of cases and deaths in each country to visualize the global impact. Stock performance of four important technology stocks (Slack, Shopify, Zoom, and Cisco) have been plotted over the period to visualize the impact of COVID-19.
