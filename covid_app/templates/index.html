Introduction

Coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19) is a contagious disease caused by severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2 (SARS-CoV-2). The first case was identified in Wuhan, China, in December 2019. The disease has since spread worldwide, leading to an ongoing pandemic. In this project we are studying the impact of COVID-19 on the World with Emphasis on North America. We have plotted a geographic world map which will show the number of cases and deaths in each country in the past year. COVID vaccines have been introduced in several countries. Our focus is on Canada and we have created a visual map using geomap to show the number os cases, deaths and vaccines given duru=ing the period of Jan 2020 to Jan 2021.
